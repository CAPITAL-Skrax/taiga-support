HipChat integration

  • 1. Configure HipChat
  • 2. Configure Taiga

HipChat: Bring your team to life.

First things first, HipChat is a huge platform with lots of features. Integration will always take time so please be patient. If you need a specific integration and you feel ready to get out your machete and start cutting into the taiga, please review our API Docs.

If you need help with a specific feature, you can always get in touch with our community through our mailing list

1. Configure HipChat

In the HipChat app select a channel and

  1. Click over Roon Action icon and select Integrations....

  2. Go to Find New tab and click in Build Your Own! .

  3. Type a name for your new integration, for example "Taiga.io", and press Create button.

  4. Now copy your Webhook URL (at the right of Send messages to this room by posting to this URL).

2. Configure Taiga

In your Taiga project:

  1. Go to Admin > Plugins > HipChat

  2. Set the HipChat Webhook URL to the copied value in the previous step and save your configuration.

You can test it, if you want, clicking over Test button and a test message shall appear in the channel.
