Importers

Taiga importers are designed for easy the migration of data from others platforms, and try to keep as much data as possible (including history and comments, when it is available), to ensure you don't miss anything when you bring your project into Taiga.

Currently we have 4 importers:

  • Trello
  • Jira 7
  • Github
  • Asana
